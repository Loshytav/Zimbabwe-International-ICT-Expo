October, 3, 2024

ZITF Hillside Road, Bulawayo, Zimbabwe

Few words about the ZIMBABWE INTERNATIONAL ICT Expo

The Annual Zimbabwe International ICT Expo serves as a convergence point for ICT developers, universities, students, and companies on a global scale. This event aims to exhibit technological advancements, stimulate innovation, promote international collaboration, and create business prospects. Featuring workshops, panel discussions, and displays of state-of-the-art products and services, the expo promotes networking, knowledge sharing, and professional growth among students. Additionally, it underscores Zimbabwe's technological progress and emphasizes the importance of collaborative efforts in driving technological innovation and economic development.

About the Expo

Welcome to the Zimbabwe International ICT Expo, the premier event focused on narrowing the digital divide by delving into Artificial Intelligence and cybersecurity. Centered on harnessing the immense capabilities of these advanced technologies, our theme aims to drive industrial growth and facilitate the realization of Vision 2030. We invite you to participate in exploring the forefront of technology, innovation, and global digital transformation.

Exhibit at the Zimbabwe International ICT Expo

The Zimbabwe International ICT Expo offers a unique platform for companies to showcase their latest technologies and services. Exhibiting connects you with a vast audience of potential customers, partners, and investors. Network with industry leaders, generate leads, and increase brand awareness. A variety of exhibitor packages are available to suit your needs. Don't miss this opportunity - secure your spot today!

Planning Your Visit to Bulawayo

Bulawayo is a vibrant city with a rich history and culture. To assist you with planning your trip, we recommend referring to reputable travel websites or contacting local travel agencies for booking flights, accommodation, and ground transportation.

Unleash a World of Opportunities at the Expo

The Zimbabwe International ICT Expo is your gateway to a wealth of business opportunities. Connect with a targeted audience of key decision-makers, industry leaders, and potential partners. Generate qualified leads, showcase your brand to a wide audience, and explore new markets. The Expo provides a dynamic platform to launch new products, gain valuable market insights, and propel your business to the forefront of the ICT sector.

Become a Partner & Drive Innovation Together

The Zimbabwe International ICT Expo thrives on collaboration. Partnering with the Expo offers a unique opportunity to align your brand with a premier ICT event and showcase your commitment to technological advancement. Enjoy significant brand exposure, networking opportunities with industry leaders, and the chance to contribute to the development of Zimbabwe's ICT sector. Explore various partnership opportunities to find the perfect fit for your organization. Let's join forces to drive innovation and shape the future of technology in Zimbabwe!
